Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to set up a space for longer-form writing for a while now, and here it is.

## What to expect

I plan to write about:

- **Neuroscience research** — thoughts on papers, methods, and open questions
- **Open-source software** — tools I'm building or using, lessons learned
- **Reproducible science** — workflows, best practices, and why it matters
- **Miscellaneous** — anything else that catches my attention

## Why a blog?

Academic papers are great for formal contributions, but there's a lot of thinking that happens in between — the kind of stuff that doesn't fit neatly into a manuscript but is still worth sharing. This blog is for that.

Stay tuned for more posts. If you have questions or want to chat about anything I write, feel free to reach out via the [contact page](/contact).
